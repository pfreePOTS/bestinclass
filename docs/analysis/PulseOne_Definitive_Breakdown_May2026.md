## G. The Hard Truth

You are technically breaking even, but the business model is broken. 
1. **Projects are a massive cash incinerator.** You are paying $39,194/month in labor to generate $25,000/month in project revenue. 
2. **Engineering talent is doing admin work.** Calkins, Froio, and Walsh are costing you $18,659/month in non-billable SG&A time.
3. **Sales is bloated.** You are spending $43,833/month on Sales to maintain a $284K/month revenue base.

**The Fix:**
You do not need to fire anyone immediately to survive, because you are at cash breakeven. But to become profitable, you must either:
A) Double project revenue immediately using the existing team (Vincent, Jeremy Roe, Harris).
B) Reallocate Calkins, Froio, and Walsh to 90%+ billable MS work, and fire the offshore MS contractors (Daniel, Dare, George) they replace.
C) Cut the non-producing sales overhead (Marcello, Gianpaolo).
