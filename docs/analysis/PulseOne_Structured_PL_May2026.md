## E. Key Takeaways

1. **The True Margin Picture:** By correctly classifying all W-2 labor and software/tools directly against the revenue they support, the overall Gross Margin is **38.2%**. This is below the BiC floor of 44.0%.
2. **The Project Drag:** The Consulting/Projects division is operating at a **-57.0% margin**, consuming $39,243 in labor (both W-2 and outsourced) to generate only $25,000 in revenue.
3. **The SG&A Bloat:** With Gross Margin at 38.2%, PulseOne cannot support an operating expense structure that consumes 46.7% of revenue. The bloat is concentrated in Sales (16.1%) and G&A (23.7%).
4. **The Cash Burn:** At the current revenue baseline of $284K/month, the company is operating at an EBITDA loss of -$24,209/month, which extends to a **cash burn of -$44,209/month** when debt and distributions are included.
