## A. Objective
This document serves as the **single source of truth** for how every PulseOne labor resource (W-2, Upwork, and contractor) is allocated across service segments, cost categories, and entities for CY 2025. All segment-level P&L calculations, COGS classifications, and margin analyses must reference this document. It supersedes all prior resource classification assumptions in other analysis documents.

## C. Current Understanding
PulseOne operates with a blended workforce of W-2 employees, Upwork freelancers, and direct 1099/B2B contractors. Prior analyses relied on assumptions for segment allocation (e.g., classifying all help desk staff as 100% Managed Services COGS). This document applies owner-confirmed allocations, separating Comprehensive Services (ARC/Bunzl staff augmentation) from discrete Consulting/Projects, and removing separate entity costs (RepScheduler and PulseOne Communications) from the operating baseline.

## G. Service Leadership Best in Class Alignment
The isolation of ARC and Bunzl staffing (Comprehensive Services) from Managed Services and Projects is required by BiC methodology. The exclusion of non-operating entity costs (RS/POC) is required for accurate EBITDA calculation. The compensation of partners at FMV means no add-back is appropriate under BiC rules.

## H. Risks and Opportunities
- **Risk:** Without exact project costing time-entry data, the exact margin split between MS and CP remains an estimate. The "invisible subsidy" of W-2 labor continues to distort project profitability.
- **Opportunity:** Clearly separating ARC/Bunzl staffing from the Help Desk shows the core MS delivery engine is highly efficient, allowing management to focus solely on fixing project margins and rationalizing S&M spend.

## I. Recommended Actions
1. Adopt this document as the definitive resource baseline for all 2025 financial modeling.
2. Ensure the bookkeeper explicitly tags RS and POC expenses in QuickBooks so they do not artificially depress PulseOne's operating margin.
3. Track Comprehensive Services (staff augmentation) on a separate P&L segment from discrete Projects.
4. Require fully-loaded labor estimates on all new project quotes (CON-06).
