## E. Decision Framework & Trigger Points

You are currently facing a ~$40K/month operating deficit. You cannot wait for the new sales team to close a $100K/month revenue gap before running out of cash. You must execute a "barbell" strategy: immediate cuts to lower the breakeven point, combined with strict 90-day ROI triggers for the sales team.

### Action 1: Right-Size Service Delivery Labor (Immediate)
You are carrying **$102,152/month** in service delivery labor. This is scaled for a $400K+ revenue company. With ARC gone and the MS base shrinking, this team is underutilized.
* **The Cut Needed:** $20,000 - $25,000/month
* **The Target:** Review the Upwork ($21K) and Payoneer ($8K) layers first. Vincent Williams is billing $9K/month primarily for projects; if project revenue is down to $100K/month, his cost is unjustified. George and Dare are costing $8K/month for Help Desk overflow; with Joel and Omar furloughed, are they still needed, or can the W-2 team absorb the volume?

### Action 2: Enforce the 90-Day Sales Trigger (August 1)
You are investing **$23,807/month** ($285K annualized) in Sales/BizDev. This is 7.5% of revenue, well above the Service Leadership Best in Class target of 4-6%.
* **The ROI Requirement:** To justify a $285K annual spend at a 5:1 LTV/CAC ratio, this team must generate **$11,500/month in new recurring MRR**, every month.
* **The Trigger Point:** By August 1 (when the 4 reps are scheduled to convert to W-2), if they have not generated at least $25,000 in *closed* new MRR, the Payoneer contracts must be terminated rather than converted. 
* **The Legacy Cut:** Marcello and Gianpaolo are costing $5,800/month and are confirmed to be producing zero pipeline. **Cut them immediately.**

### Action 3: Audit the $74K "Unseen" Overhead (Next 14 Days)
The $74K in non-payroll expenses is a black box. If $30K of this is software COGS, there is still $44K in pure overhead.
* **The Action:** Pull a detailed P&L for April 2026 showing exactly what makes up the $74,000 difference between the $131K payroll and the $205K total expense line. 
* **The Target:** Identify $5,000 - $10,000 in immediate software/tool consolidation.
